Frequently Asked Questions

What is the size guide for your clothing?

You can find our size guide on each product page. It includes measurements for different styles to help you pick the right size. If you're unsure, feel free to contact us for assistance!

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, we will send you an email with a tracking number. You can use that number to track your order on the carrier's website.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees and delivery times vary depending on your location. Check our shipping policy for more details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and other secure payment methods. During checkout, you can choose your preferred payment option.

Can I change or cancel my order?

Once an order is placed, it is processed quickly. However, if you need to make changes or cancel, please contact us immediately. We will do our best to accommodate your request, but we cannot guarantee changes after the order has been shipped.

How do I return or exchange an item?

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy. To return or exchange an item, please follow the instructions in our Returns & Exchanges page, and make sure the item is in new condition with all tags attached.

Are your clothes ethically made?

Yes, we take sustainability and ethical practices seriously. We ensure that our clothing is produced in factories that follow fair labor practices, and we work with brands that prioritize ethical sourcing and sustainability.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! We offer gift cards in various denominations. You can purchase them on our website and use them during checkout for any future orders.

How can I get in touch with customer service?

You can contact our customer service team through our Contact Us page, or by emailing us at support@example.com. We're here to help!

What do I do if I received a damaged item?

If you received a damaged item, please contact us immediately. We will assist you with a return or exchange, and we will cover the return shipping costs. Please provide photos of the damage when contacting us.
